My name is Austin Krauss. I like building things (or helping build things) that move the ball forward. What ball? It changes, depending on the project. However, I find the greatest fulfillment when "ball" is synonymous with "helping others accomplish personal and/or socially-oriented goals".

For the reasons stated above, I'm building LifeStyleDesign. I hope that people will use it to think more deeply. For instance, I recently wrote a 2-3 year plan for myself, which detailed all the personal, career, and educational goals I wish to accomplish. I then passed this text into the application and was able to see hidden emotions in my text, which shed further light on the way I perceive events in my life - consciously or not. That's pretty cool, and I recommend that others try it out!
